About Us

Our Mission

Helping you and your friends store and share your favorite recipes! We know how hard it is to:

  • Find recipes that fit your dietary needs
  • Store recipes in a way that lets you avoid ads in the future
  • Share your creation with friends and family without getting sucked into Instagram or Facebook

So we created "Dara's Recipe Book" to help everyone store and share their recipes!

Founders

Dara Nikolova

Dara is a Kellogg MBA student with management consulting experience from her time at BCG. At BCG she focused on growth strategy and due diligence work for Fortune 500 companies and PE firms. Dara has a passion for food and hopes to share her favorite recipes with her friends and family!
